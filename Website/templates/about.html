About Us

FastTrack is a mini project developed as part of our semester coursework. It focuses on optimizing delivery routes and improving logistics efficiency using intelligent algorithms and real-time tracking.

Guidance & Institution

This project has been developed under the guidance of Anu Bonia Francis at the Department of Computer Science, Rajiv Gandhi Institute of Technology, Kottayam.

Motivation & Goals

Our motivation behind FastTrack was to address real-world logistics challenges and improve delivery efficiency through technology. We aimed to learn and implement cutting-edge tools like Flutter, Firebase, Google Maps API, and intelligent sorting algorithms to create an efficient, scalable solution.
